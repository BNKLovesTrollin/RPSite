Frequently Asked Questions

Here in this section you can clarify all your questions you have. If you have more, you can join our Discord and ask.


Can I chargeback any plugin?

No, you can't chargeback any plugin. If you make any chargeback, your license will be removed permanently and you will be banned from our Discord.

Are my plugins permanent?

Yes, your product will always be permanent as long as you comply with our Terms of Service found on our Discord.

Can I increse my license IPs limit?

Yes, you can increase the maximum number of IPs in your license, as long as you give valid reasons why you want to increase them.

Which payment methods do we accept?

Our main payment methods are PayPal and Cryptocurrencies. But we can offer a variety of payments depending on the country in which you are living, you can check which payment methods are available in your country through Tebex.
